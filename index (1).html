How It Works

A simple, streamlined process connecting donors, blood banks, and hospitals.

01

Donor Registration

Donors register online or at the blood bank with their personal and medical details.

02

Screening & Donation

Donors are screened for eligibility. Successful donors contribute blood which is typed and stored.

03

Inventory Updated

The blood bank inventory is instantly updated with the new units, expiry tracking enabled.

04

Hospital Request

Hospitals submit blood requests through the portal. Requests are matched to available inventory.

05

Dispatch & Delivery

Approved requests are dispatched to hospitals with tracking and receipt confirmation.
